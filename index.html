About Brûlé

Where craft
meets comfort.

We started Brûlé with one obsession: what happens between the farm and your first sip. That journey — spanning continents, climates, and careful hands — is what we honour in every roast.

Our baristas are artisans. Our beans are traceable. Our space is designed for the kind of stillness that only a perfect cup can bring.

The Brûlé way

Slow is
the point.

01

Sourced with intent

We work directly with farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — long-term relationships built on fair trade and transparency.

02

Roasted in-house

Our roaster runs every morning at 6am. By the time you walk in, the beans in your cup were roasted hours earlier — never days.

03

Brewed with precision

Temperature, grind size, extraction time — every variable is dialled in. Every cup is tasted before it reaches you.
